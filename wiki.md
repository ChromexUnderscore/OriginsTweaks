# OriginsTweaks

Welcome to the **OriginsTweaks Wiki**! This wiki is designed to familiarize you with the origins, powers, and gameplay changes OriginsTweaks provides.

OriginsTweaks builds upon the **Origins Mod** and it's default Origins, expanding their functionality with reworked and new abilities.  
OriginsTweaks' goal is to enhance the Origins Mod experience by offering players a twist with in-depth abilities and Quality-of-Life changes for their favorite Origin.

In this wiki, you'll find:
- **Information about Origins Mod Changes**: Find out about the changes to abilities and playstyles of each default origin.
- **Details on the new OriginsTweaks Abilities**: Understand how each original ability works, including cooldowns, effects and more.
- **Playstyle Tips and Tricks**: Learn about how to use the abilities of each origin, including tips and tricks to maximize their potential.

# Arachnid

The Arachnid origin grants players spider-like abilities, allowing them to climb walls, use webs for mobility, and use poisonous attacks in combat. This origin is ideal for players who enjoy extensive mobility and tactical combat.

## Abilities

### Master of Webs [C]
- Added a **Webshot Ability**:
    - Fire webs with the **[Primary] Key** to grapple to blocks or mobs within 16 blocks.
    - Has **7 Charges**, regenerating over 35 seconds.
    - Charges also refill after sleeping or respawning.
- **Cobweb Crafting**: Cobwebs can be crafted using 9 strings instead of 2.
    - Sneaking while mining cobwebs is faster and guarantees 9 string drops.

### Venom [OT]
- Melee attacks have a **25% chance** to inflict **Poison II** for 5 seconds.
    - **Cooldown**: 5 seconds.
- Switch between **Climbing** and **Venom** abilities using the **[Secondary] Key**.

### Climbing [C]
- Climbing walls no longer reduces mining speed.
- Switch between **Climbing** and **Venom** abilities using the **[Secondary] Key**.

### Arthropod [C]
- Arachnids are approximately **1.5 blocks tall** and can fit into 1-block gaps when sneaking.
- Fall damage is only taken after falling more than **16 blocks**.

### Fragile [U]
- You have 3 less hearts of health than humans.

### Carnivore [C]
- Your diet is restricted to meat, you can't eat vegetables.
    - Using Fabric API Conventional tags for support of most modded foods.

## Playstyle Tips
- Use the **Webshot** Ability to quickly traverse difficult terrain or cycle in and out of combat.
- Take advantage of your size to navigate tight spaces to avoid enemies.
- Switch between **Climbing** and **Venom** abilities depending on the situation.
    - Toggling off **Climbing** can be helpful to not get stuck in narrow spaces such as doorways.
- Craft cobwebs for traps, mob farms or to decorate your builds.


# Avian

The Avian origin provides players with quality of life traits such as faster movement, bonuses for farming crops and the ability to tame certain animals for free. This origin is perfect for players who enjoy taking it easy.

## Abilities

### Featherweight [C]
- Use the **[Secondary] Key** to toggle the Featherweight Ability instead of sneaking.

### Animal Friendship [OT]
- Activate with the **[Primary] Key** to tame certain animals.
    - **Cooldown**: 20 minutes.
    - **Tameable Animals**: Wolves, Cats, Parrots, Horses, Donkeys, Mules, Llamas.
- Some Animals can't be tamed, but have a "trust" mechanic.
    - Trusting animals won't run from the Avian and will aid the Avian in combat.
    - **Trusting Animals**: Axolotls, Foxes, Ocelots.

### Green Thumb [OT]
- Harvesting crops with a hoe gives bonus item drops, which stacks with Fortune Enchantments.
    - Crops also have a 50% chance to drop golden variants, including **Glistering Melons**.
- **Glistering Melons** are edible and restore **6 Hunger Points** and **4 Saturation Points**.

### Tailwind [U]
- You are a little bit quicker on foot than others.

### Like Air [U]
- Modifiers to your walking speed also apply while you're airborne.

### Vegetarian [C]
- You can't digest any meat.
    - Using Fabric API Conventional tags for support of most modded foods.

### Fresh Air [U]
- When sleeping, your bed needs to be at an altitude of at least 86 blocks, so you can breathe fresh air.

### Oviparous [U]
- Whenever you wake up in the morning, you will lay an egg.

## Playstyle Tips
- Activate the **Featherweight** ability for slow falling while exploring, and deactivate it to enable falling Critical Hits in combat or navigate tight spaces.
- Use **Animal Friendship** to tame animals without the need of the right item.
- Craft a hoe to increase your crop yield and gain easy access to golden variants via the **Green Thumb** Ability.
- Use **Glistering Melons** as a reliable food option during exploration or combat.

# Blazeborn

The Blazeborn origin grants players fire-based abilities, making them formidable in combat and resistant to heat. This origin rewards aggressive playstyles and players who don't mind dangerous environments such as the nether.

## Abilities

### Burning Wrath [C]
- Activate with the **[Primary] Key** to engulf yourself in flames for **30 seconds**.
    - Light **Campfires**, fuel **Furnaces**, and **Brewing Stands** while on fire.
    - Gain a **+25% Damage Bonus** and set enemies on fire for **6 seconds** with melee hits.
    - **Cooldown**: 60 seconds.

### Firecharged Projectile [OT]
- Fire a burst of **3 Fireballs** with the **[Secondary] Key**:
    - Fireballs have **no gravity**.
    - **Cooldown**: 10 seconds.

### Fire Immunity [C]
- Move slightly faster through **lava** and swim when submerged.
- Gain improved **Lava Vision**, comparable with the Vision provided by **Fire Resistance**.

### Nether Inhabitant [U]
- Your natural spawn will be in the Nether.

### Hotblooded [U]
- Due to your hot body, venoms burn up, making you immune to poison and hunger status effects.

### Hydrophobia [U]

- You receive damage over time while in contact with water.
    - You may find a new enchantment called "Water Protection", one piece of equipment with the highest level will suffice to negate water DoT.

## Playstyle Tips
- Use **Burning Wrath** to improve damage output during combat or use utility blocks without fuel.
- Take advantage of **Fire Immunity** to navigate lava-filled areas with ease.
- Use **Firecharged Projectile** to attack enemies from a distance; especially useful in open spaces.
- Be mindful of the cooldowns to time your abilities effectively during combat.

# Elytrian

The Elytrian origin grants players flight-related abilities, making them masters of aerial combat and mobility. This origin is perfect for players who enjoy soaring through the skies and engaging in strategic combat from above.

## Abilities

### Gift of the Skies [C]
- Press the **[Primary] Key** to ascend upwards using **25 Stamina**.
- Max Stamina: **600**.
    - Rest while gliding, on the ground, sleep or respawn to refill stamina.
- Wearing **13+ Armor** weakens the ability and increases stamina costs:
    - +50 Stamina for launching.
    - +25 Stamina for ascending.
    - **20+ Armor** disables launching and ascending entirely.

### Companion Mounting [OT]
- Other players can **right-click a sneaking Elytrian** to mount them.
    - Elytrians can **kick off passengers** by pressing the **[Secondary] Key**.
    - Passengers are also dismounted if:
        - The Elytrian crashes into terrain.
        - The Elytrian is hit by a projectile.
- This ability was contributed by **AnimGoated**.

### Aerial Combatant [C]
- Grants a stacking melee damage bonus relative on current flight velocity, up to 3x.

### Claustrophobia [U]
- Being somewhere with a low ceiling for too long will weaken you and make you slower.

### Brittle Bones [U]
- You take more damage from falling and flying into blocks.

### Wing Options [OT]
- Elytrians can choose between:
    - **Vanilla Elytra Rendering**.
    - **Custom Wing Textures**.
    - **No Wing Rendering**.
- A template for custom wings is available in **Modrinth** and **GitHub releases**.

## Playstyle Tips
- Use the active ability in **Gift of the Skies** to maintain your altitude while flying.
- Take advantage of **Aerial Combatant** to deal heavy damage when rushing past someone at high speeds.
    - Like in Vanilla Minecraft, **Maces** will not benefit from smashing attacks while in elytra flight.
- Be mindful of your **Stamina Bar** and avoid wearing heavy armor to maximize flight time.
    - Optimal Flight Armor: Iron Helmet paired with Chainmail Chestplate, Leggings and Boots.
- Use **Companion Mounting** to quickly transport allies from A to B.



# Enderian

The Enderian origin grants players abilities inspired by the Endermen, granting teleportation, evasion , and unique physical traits. This origin is ideal for players who enjoy mobility and outmaneuvering the enemy.

## Abilities

### Teleportation [C]
- Teleporting via Ender Pearl grants a brief **invincibility window** against suffocation damage.

### Slender Body [C]
- Enderians are **2.5 blocks tall** and gain a **50% increase in reach**.
- They can **Silk Touch** blocks while not holding any tools.
- Automatically **dodge projectiles** by teleporting to a random block within an **8-block radius**:
    - **Cooldown**: 5 seconds.
    - Evasive Teleportation avoids landing mid-air, on top of bedrock and into fluids.

### Hydrophobia [U]
- You receive damage over time while in contact with water.
    - You may find a new enchantment called "Water Protection", one piece of equipment with the highest level will suffice to negate water DoT.

### Scared of Gourds [U]
- You are afraid of pumpkins. For good reason.
    - You cannot eat pumpkin-based foods and Players wearing a Pumpkin Helmet are invisible to you.

## Playstyle Tips
- Use **Teleportation** to navigate challenging terrain or escape dangerous situations via free enderpearls.
- Take advantage of your **increased height and reach** to interact with blocks or attack mobs that are out of reach for other origins.
- Time the cooldown of your **Projectile Dodge** ability to avoid incoming ranged attacks. and catch your enemy off-guard.
- Be mindful of your taller stature when navigating tight spaces or structures with low ceilings.


# Feline

The Feline origin grants players cat-like traits, enhanced agility and extra combos in combat. This origin is ideal for players who enjoy mobility and fast paced melee combat.

## Abilities

### Natural Hunter [OT]
- Felines can deal an additional armor piercing strike using a free off-hand, but must regularly resharpen their claws:
    - Sharpen claws by interacting with specific blocks in order:
        - **Wood Tier**: Wood, Logs, Wool, or Carpet.
        - **Stone Tier**: Smooth Stone, Polished Granite, Polished Andesite, Polished Diorite or Polished Tuff.
        - **Iron Tier**: Iron Blocks.
        - **Diamond Tier**: Diamond Blocks.
    - Claws may deal damage ranging from 0-8 depending on the current claw sharpness meter.
    - Off-hand attacks activate automatically after half a second if the target is still in range, and inflict less knockback, making them effective for combos.
- Food made from fish restores an additional **50% Hunger Points** and **100% Saturation Points**.

### Acrobatics [C]
- Hold the **[Primary] Key** to charge and release to peform a big leap.
    - Jump height ranges from **2 to 6 blocks**, depending on charge level, with added forward momentum.
    - **Cooldown**: 4 seconds.

### Weak Arms [C]
- Breaking stone blocks is **50% slower**, and some tough stone blocks cannot be broken without the **Strength Potion Effect** or a pickaxe with **Efficiency**.
- **Affected Stone Blocks**:
    - Stone, Stone Ores, Ancient Debris, Granite, Diorite, Andesite, Sandstone, Smooth Sandstone, Red Sandstone, Smooth Red Sandstone, Smooth Basalt, Calcite, Block of Amethyst, Budding Amethyst, Tuff, Dripstone Block, Netherrack, Basalt, Magma Block, End Stone.
- **Tough Stone Blocks**:
    - Deepslate, Reinforced Deepslate, Deepslate Ores, Obsidian, Crying Obsidian, Blackstone, Gilded Blackstone.

### Velvet Paws [U]
- Your footsteps don't cause any vibrations which could otherwise be picked up by nearby lifeforms.

### Nine Lives [U]
- You have 1 less heart of health than humans.

### Catlike Appearance [U]
- Creepers are scared of you and will only explode if you attack them first.

### Nocturnal [C]
- Activate or deactivate Night Vision using the **[Secondary] Key**.

## Playstyle Tips
- Use **Natural Hunter** to deal damage with rapid melee strikes and keep your enemies at bay with knockback effects.
- Resharpen your claws by sharpening them on the appropriate blocks; Wood / Wool -> Polished Stone -> Iron Block -> Diamond Block.
- Prioritize fish-based food for a longer lasting meal.
- Take advantage of the **Acrobatic Leap** to navigate terrain, jump gaps or escape combat situations.
- Strategically lure creepers into a group of enemies and provoke them into exploding.
- Be mindful of your **Weak Arms** when mining and focus on gathering resources that don't require breaking tough stone blocks.
    - Use a Pickaxe enchanted with Efficiency or use Strength Potions to cancel the effects of **Weak Arms**.


# Merling

The Merling origin grants players aquatic abilities, making them masters of underwater exploration and combat. This origin is ideal for players who enjoy a good challenge in the ocean.

## Abilities

### Gills [C]
- You can breathe underwater, but not on land.
- Merlings have a **Body Moisture Bar** that allows staying out of the water for a short while:
    - **600 Units** of Moisture last for roughly **10 minutes** in the Overworld and the End, but only **5 minutes** in the Nether.
    - Regain Moisture by:
        - Jumping in water.
        - Sitting in a water cauldron.
        - Drinking a potion or bottle of water (**+60 Units** / * 1min per bottle).
    - **Conduit Power** works out of water, extends Moisture duration and delays drying out:
        - Lasts **5 minutes** when near an activated underwater conduit.
        - Prevents the Body Moisture Bar from expiring while active.

### Aquadynamic [OT]
- Merlings can **craft Tridents**.
    - [Crafting Recipe](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/FRBxogjw/images/2d0819e26f5afce6716b9584f21c022b9175bc86.png).
- **Trident Attacks** (melee and ranged) deal increased damage when both the merling as well as the target are underwater.

### Fins [C]
- Merlings can **dash underwater** while swimming:
    - Stacks with **Depth Strider** enchantments.
    - **Cooldown**: 5 seconds.

### Wet Eyes [U]
- Your vision underwater is perfect.
    - Toggle with the **[Primary] Key**.

### Aqua Affinity [U]
- You may break blocks underwater as others do on land.

### Like Water [U]
 - When underwater, you do not sink to the ground unless you want to.

### Aquatic [C]
- The Merling's **natural spawn point** is in ocean biomes.
- They are part of Aquatic group, so they take more damage from the Impaling Enchantment. 

## Playstyle Tips
- Keep an eye on your **Body Moisture Bar** and plan your exploration around water sources.
- Carry bottles of water as a backup to quickly refill your Moisture Bar when away from water sources.
- Use your aquatic abilities to explore ocean monuments, shipwrecks, and other underwater structures effectively.
- Craft a Trident for underwater combat without the need to farm Drowneds.
- Use the **Fins Ability** to quickly move through bodies of water.
- Combine **Depth Strider** enchantments with your **Fins Ability** for unparalleled swim speeds.
- Use **Conduit Power** to massively extend your safe time out of water.


# Phantom

The Phantom origin grants players ghostly abilities, allowing them to phase through blocks, detect enemies through walls and absorb soul power from defeated enemies. This origin is ideal for players who enjoy stealth and unrestricted movement.

## Abilities

### Soulbound [OT]
- Introduces a **Soul Energy Bar** required for certain abilities:
    - **100 Units** of Soul Energy regenerate over **50 seconds**.
    - Gain additional Soul Energy when near **Soulfire**.
    - Recharge Soul Energy by killing enemies based on their max health:
        - **0.5 - 5 Hearts**: +5 Soul Energy.
        - **5.5 - 10 Hearts**: +25 Soul Energy.
        - **10.5 - 50 Health**: +50 Soul Energy.
        - **50+ Health (Boss Mobs)**: Fully restores Soul Energy.

### Phantom Form [C]
- Enter a **Phantom Form** to become invisible and enable phasing:
    - No longer requires Hunger but consumes **0.5 Soul Energy per second** when not actively phasing.

### Phase [C]
- Smoothly phase through blocks without stopping at each layer:
    - **Partial Phasing** restricts sprinting, swimming, and elytra flight.
    - **Full Phasing** additionally prevents attacking and interacting with blocks.
- Consumes **1.5 Soul Energy per second**.

### Spectral Wail [OT]
- Highlight mobs and players with a **Glowing Effect** only for yourself:
    - Activate with the **[Secondary] Key**.
    - Affects mobs and players within a **32-block radius** for **30 seconds**.
    - **Cooldown**: 2 Minutes.

### Aurophobia [C]
- Phantoms are repelled by gold:
    - Cannot eat **Golden Apples**, **Enchanted Golden Apples**, or **Golden Carrots**.
    - Cannot wear **Golden Armor**.
    - **Golden Tools** prevent mining and block interactions.
    - Cannot phase through **blocks made of pure gold**.

### Photoallergic [U]
- You begin to burn in daylight if you are not invisible.

### Translucent [U]
- Your skin is translucent.

## Playstyle Tips
- Keep your **Soul Energy Gauge** stocked to access Phantom Form and Phase abilities.
- Use **Spectral Wail** to subtly locate hidden players or mobs in dark areas or behind Walls and use **Phantom Form** to avoid a fight or catch them by suprise.
- Avoid gold-based items and blocks to prevent restrictions on your abilities.
- Be cautious when phasing, as you cannot attack or interact with the environment until you resurface.

# Shulk

The Shulk origin grants players abilities inspired by Shulkers, adding abilities related to defense, Gravitational Manipulation and a bigger Inventory. This origin is ideal for players who enjoy tanky playstyles and tossing their enemies around.

## Abilities

### Hoarder [U]
- You have access to an additional 9 slots of inventory, which keep the items on death.
    - Access using the **[Secondary] Key**.

### Sturdy Skin [C]
- You have a permanent **+3 Natural Armor (1.5 Plates)**.
- While Sneaking, your natural armor grows by **1 Armor point every 2 seconds**, capping at **+10 total Armor**.
- Taking a hit stronger than **6 HP (3 Hearts)** removes 2 Bonus Armor (1 Plate) and **pauses shell regeneration for 30 seconds**.

### Strong Arms [U]
- You are strong enough to break natural stones without using a pickaxe.

### Unwieldy [U]
- The way your hands are formed provide no way of holding a shield upright.

### Large Appetite [U]
- You exhaust much quicker than others, thus requiring you to eat more.

## Playstyle Tips
- Safeguard all your important items in your Shulker Inventory, which persists through death.
- Build Armor via **Sturdy Skin** to absorb damage and maintain a strong defense in combat.
- Use your quick hunger as an Advantage to heal more, but make sure to keep a stockpile of food when exploring or engaging in combat.
